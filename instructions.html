Instructions

Objective

The goal of blackjack is to beat the dealer's hand without going over 21.

Card Values

Number cards (2-10) are worth their face value.

Face cards (Jack, Queen, King) are each worth 10 points.

Ace can be worth 1 or 11 points, whichever is better for the player.

Joker card: If you receive a Joker, you automatically win the round.

Game Play

Each player, including the dealer, is dealt two cards.

Players can either "hit" (take another card) or "stand" (keep their current total).

The dealer must hit until their cards total 17 or higher.

If a player's total exceeds 21, they "bust" and lose the game.

If a player's total is higher than the dealer's without exceeding 21, they win.

Winning

A blackjack (Ace + 10-point card) wins automatically.

If both the player and dealer have the same total, it's a "push" (tie).

If a player receives a Joker card, they automatically win the round.

Enjoy playing Jokerjack!
